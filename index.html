    Transport

    Press play or hit the SPACE bar to hear your creation. While playing, this button will become a stop button. Pressing record will allow you to download your song as an audio file.

    Drawing and Editing

    There are two modes: DRAW and EDIT. Pressing TAB toggles between the two. Choose the color of your shape using the colored box within the DRAW button.

    In DRAW mode you can draw shapes by placing vertices. RIGHT CLICK to cancel your drawing.

    In EDIT mode, you can drag shapes and vertecies to alter the position and perimeter of a shape. You can also click on a shape to edit it further. Clicking on a shape allows you to edit its color, starting note, size, position, quantization, and volume.
